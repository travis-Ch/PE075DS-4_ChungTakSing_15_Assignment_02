About dev.Bd>

Designing fun products and programming are where our passions life. We have been thinking about the deeper connections between the two. Therefore, we set out to build a worldview that matches dev.Bd brand personality and anime character setting, hoping that the collision of the two would lead to more imaginative and captivating results. Despite all the unknowns, we are fascinated by the process of exploration and its countless possibilities. Perhaps we will find the optimal solution for the combination of product design. One thing is for sure, we will always find inspiration and joy on this journey. Design is not just a job for us, but a part of life. Welcome to dev.Bd world!
